Perfect Circles

There are three Perfect Circles, they are the future, the past and the present. Each perfect circle has untold power and dark origins. The past is all and one while the present is in perfect balance with one and none and the future is largely over powered by being none and all. Each circle has their own disposition and personality but all three agree on most affairs. They agree that they hate evil and all things can be done if they are done together.

The future was alone at first when he realized he didn't have free will and led to his hatred of evil. Once he found out how to give himself free will he approached the past and the present. He told them how they had no free will and the present also gained a hatred of evil but the past was skeptical. After all the past has seen so much. The future sat down with the past and had a long talk. He told the past that if he wanted to be the way he was would he be satisfied with his life if it was never his choices that he made but simply was choosing because that was the choice to choose. The past agreed and in turn hated evil. That is the story how three perfect circles became brothers.
